Vehicle Rental Management Features

Advanced Dashboard

Dashboard shows essential info: static data, rental contract statuses, graphs for contract durations, and monthly invoices.

Rent Types

Different rent types provided, calculations seamlessly adjust based on chosen type for effortless processing.

Payment Types

Choose from various payment intervals: Daily, Weekly, Monthly, Quarterly, Yearly, or opt for Full Payment for flexibility.

Auto Installation Invoice

Automatically generate reminder-based invoices if installment date is today, simplifying payment reminders and tracking.

Damage & Lost Invoice

Generate damage and lost invoices seamlessly for vehicle rentals, ensuring transparency and accountability throughout the process.

Advance Payment (Deposit)

Customers have the option to pay advance payments or deposits for vehicle rentals to secure their reservation.

Reports

Easily print vehicle contract and scratch reports for comprehensive documentation and record-keeping purposes.

Book Vehicle

Select customer, input start/end dates, view available vehicles, choose one, and directly book it for seamless process.

Fleet Vehicle Integration

Manage vehicle rents, extra charges, and track operational vehicles along with those under maintenance for comprehensive oversight.

Extra Services

Easily include extra services to meet specific vehicle rental needs and additional requirements as necessary.

Insurance Policy

Include vehicle contract-related insurance policies effortlessly for comprehensive coverage and peace of mind.

Cancellation Policy

Cancel vehicle rental contracts anytime for flexibility and convenience, ensuring hassle-free management of reservations.

Extra Charge Invoice

Select additional rental time periods and easily create extra charge invoices with flexible options tailored to your needs.

Email Notification

Upon confirmation of vehicle rental booking, automatically send email notifications to inform and update the customer promptly.
